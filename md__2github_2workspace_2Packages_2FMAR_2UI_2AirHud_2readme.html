Air LED

The Air LED is a HUD element that stays rigidly affixed to the user's point of view, showing 4 small, colored circles in the bottom-left corner of the user's view. The 4 circles, which disappear from right to left, represent how much air remains in the local client of instance of ClientAirBroadcaster.

  • If all 4 circles are lit, remaining air is above 75%.
  • If the green circle is absent (3 remain lit) remaining air is above 50%.
  • If the yellow circle is absent (2 remain lit) remaining air is above 25%.
  • If the second rid circle is absent (only 1 remains lit) remaining air is above 0%, but below 25%.
  • If all 4 circles are absent, air is completely depleted.

Setup

Drag the AirLED prefab anywhere into the root level of the scene.
